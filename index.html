A diverse backround, the building blocks to a quality developer.

I Started my professional career as a Hotel F&B manager right out of college, that eventually lead to Executive Chef and General Manger. I am so thankful for the time I spent in hotels and restaurants. “It was the best of times; it was the worst of times”. I was lucky enough to use my years of restaurant experience to pivot my career into food service supply sales. I spent quite a few years helping my former colleagues save money and time with superior, more efficient products. I thought I would be doing this for the rest of my life, software development would always just be a hobby. Very similar to coaching sailing, I would always pursue it, but it didn’t seem like It would be my career. Until the pandemic of 2020. My employer almost shut down, laying off 85% of their workforce, including me. This allowed me to enroll in We Can Code IT’s coding bootcamp and pursue my passion for software development.
